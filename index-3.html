Canyon "Fairy Tale"

Secret wild locations

A sign on the side of the main road on the southern shore of Issyk-Kul near the village of Kadzhi-Sai will help you find your way to the Fairy Tale. If you want to spend more time there, you can use the yurt camps at the entrance to the canyon!

On the southern shore of Lake Issyk-Kul lies a colorful canyon with striking jagged cliffs formed by thousands of years of ice, water and wind erosion. Fairytale Canyon has many local legends associated with it, its central rock formation resembles the spine of a dragon with two parallel vertical rock outcroppings that flank the main trail.
