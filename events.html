Nomads game

09 September 2016

The World Nomad Games is the largest international project of the Kyrgyz State. It was initiated by the Kyrgyz Government in 2012 in order to revive and preserve the culture of the nomadic civilization.

Girl Chasing

11 January 2020 at 7.00 PM

Kyz kumai game is one of the oldest interesting games of the Kyrgyz people. Kyrgyz people often played this game when the bride and groom were getting married, at dinners, and on holidays, making fun of the crowd.

Tug of war

20 January 2020 at 8.00 PM

The players are divided into two groups and the rope is placed at the place where the game is held. A colored cloth is tied in the middle of the rope, three horizontal lines are drawn on the ground, the distance from the middle line to the outer lines is up to 2 meters. The players are divided into two groups. The players of each group hold on to one side of the rope after one player and the rest of the players on the other side of the rope after another player.

Children's conflict

17 September 2020 at 9.00 PM

Kid tussle, blue wolf is the national horse game of Kyrgyz. Children's fights have been held at grand ceremonies, holidays, and feasts since ancient times. Because it is a complicated, dangerous and random game, referees (referees) were appointed from respected and experienced people between the competitors, and the game was held according to their decision.

Five stones

31 October 2020 at 8.00 PM

Besh kash, toptash is a chess-like game of young children, mostly girls. More than 2 children participate in playing it. One of the players from the 1st side scatters the stones in his hand on the ground. Take a stone from the middle and throw it up, until it comes from above, take a stone from the ground to meet the thrown stone.
